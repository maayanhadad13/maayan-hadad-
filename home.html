HITיריד

Educational game

In this game the player is inside a fair. The game has several questions that needs to answer
within a set time limit of 30 seconds. The player's goal is to answer all the questions in order to get
enough water to fill the tube, that’s how the ball will rise up until it floats beyond the finish line.
The answers options are displayed on buckets and only after the player drags the bucket into the tube he
will know if the answer is correct. Any correct answer will fill the tube with water and bring the player
closer to achieving the goal. This game system can fit any subject and learners with a basic technology
orientation however the design concept in the game is best adapted for children and youth.

Cyberbullying

Modern interpretation for famous art piece

Me and my project partner chose Edvard Munch famous art piece "The Scream".
Our interpterion describe scream as well, a modern scream- cyberbullying.
Unfortunately, in addition to the countless benefits that technology gives us,
it also brings with it quite a few problems. That's why we decided we wanted to express
one of the most critical problems created by the technological age we live in.
The ability of people to hide behind the screens and hurt other people is a phenomenon
that must be eradicated as soon as possible!
